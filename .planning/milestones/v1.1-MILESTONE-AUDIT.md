## Critical Gap: Kaavya Completion Detection Bug

**Root cause:** `useReader.ts` stores `currentPage` as 0-indexed (0 to N-1), but three Phase 10 components check `rs.currentPage === rs.totalPages` which is always false since `currentPage` < `totalPages`.

**Files affected:**
- `src/app/components/QuizView.tsx` (line ~259)
- `src/app/components/MetricsDashboard.tsx` (line ~40)
- `src/app/components/CompactRankBadge.tsx` (line ~22)

**Fix:** Change all three checks to `rs.currentPage >= rs.totalPages - 1`

**Requirements impacted:** GAME-02, GAME-03, GAME-04, METR-04
